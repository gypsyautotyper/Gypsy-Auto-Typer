About Gypsy AutoTyper

Gypsy AutoTyper is a solo student project built with one goal in mind: helping other students save time and sanity. Born out of frustration with repetitive, boring assignments and tasks, this tool was created to work around copy-paste detection while keeping things simple, effective, and — most importantly — free. Unlike other auto-typers that are either too complicated or hidden behind a paywall, Gypsy AutoTyper is designed to be accessible to everyone.

It’s still a work in progress, and feedback is not just welcome — it’s essential. Please use the button below to submit your thoughts.
